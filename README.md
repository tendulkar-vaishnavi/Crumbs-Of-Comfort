Overview

Crumbs of Comfort is a multi-vendor bakery marketplace Android application that connects customers, local bakeries, delivery partners, and administrators on a single platform. The application streamlines bakery ordering, custom cake requests, order management, payments, and delivery tracking.

Problem Statement

Many local bakeries rely on phone calls, messaging apps, and walk-in customers to manage orders. This creates challenges such as:

1. Inefficient order tracking
2. Limited online visibility
3. Difficulty managing custom cake requests
4. Lack of delivery coordination
5. Fragmented customer experience

Crumbs of Comfort provides a centralized digital platform to address these challenges.

Business Objectives:
1. Enable bakeries to expand their online presence
2. Simplify bakery ordering and customization
3. Improve order and delivery management
4. Enhance customer convenience
5. Support business growth through digital operations
